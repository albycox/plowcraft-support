PlowCraft

Farm Puzzle Adventure

PlowCraft is a cozy farming puzzle game where you plow, plant, water, and harvest your way through increasingly challenging Shikaku puzzles. Unlock new crops, expand your farm, and enjoy a relaxing gameplay experience.

FAQ

  • How do I restore my purchases? Open Settings in the app and tap "Restore Purchases" to recover any previous in-app purchases.
  • How do I unlock new crops and rows? Play through daily puzzles to earn coins and unlock new content, or use the "Unlock Everything" option in Settings.
  • Is my progress saved? Yes, your game progress is saved locally on your device and synced via iCloud.
